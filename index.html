About Us

Florida Wire Fabricators is a family-run business in Winter Haven, Florida. Doug and Tony Fields, two brothers, own and operate the company. Since the company's inception in 2006, we have treated every customer as if they were a member of our family. We are proud to be a small business in central Florida that is helping to shape the agricultural industry in Florida and across the country. We provide a wide range of wire products to large orchid suppliers, citrus grove farmers, and make custom products for several companies. We take pride in our Florida roots and helping our local economy, but we also ship all over the country to make a national impact. We stay current on the state of the agricultural industry and modern manufacturing techniques in order to provide superior products at a competitive price. Contact us today to learn how we can help you improve your business and supply you with the products you need.
